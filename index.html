A Bit About Me

I'm a Master's student in Computer Science and Engineering at Politecnico di Milano, passionate about scalable cloud systems, big data analytics, and machine learning. I enjoy exploring these areas and building software that actually solves real-world problems efficiently.

I've worked with large-scale cloud and big data systems, managed containerized microservices with Docker and Kubernetes, processed huge datasets with Dask, and created deep learning projects like fingerprint segmentation in PyTorch. I also published 'Tinysign: Sign Language Recognition in Low-Resolution Settings' (SIViP, 2024), where I introduced pose-conditioned super-resolution and a low-res benchmark dataset for recognition successfully.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy exploring tech trends, testing new tools, and collaborating with people who are as curious about technology as I am personally.

Looking ahead, I want to focus on scalable cloud architectures, big data analytics, and machine learning solutions that have a real impact in the tech world globally.
